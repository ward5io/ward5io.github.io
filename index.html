Become a Software Developer

Ward 5 is 13-week immersive software development training program building a community of developers, engineers and hackers.

Weeks 1-4

Weeks 1-4 are structured deep-dives into software and web development, learning JavaScript, and how the MEAN Stack puzzle fits together.

These are critical weeks to build the foundation of good programming practices and beginning to conceptualize models and turn them into code.

Weeks 5-8

Weeks 5-8 we take your training wheels off and you start writing code on your own and in pairs. We treat these weeks as if you were just hired.

You'll focus on completing your personal projects, building ambitious group projects, and begin networking with our employer partners. Be sure to make a good first impression!

Weeks 9-13

The final leg of the course is your time to shine! You'll compelte individual and group projects and push them live to the web!

Why JavaScript?
Why MEAN Stack?

The web industry waits for no developer or technology. The future of web development has moved towards sophisticated, responsive and dynamic "single-page" applications running in the browser. Apps like Gmail, Asana, Trello, Facebook and Google Maps have redefined what users expect from "Web Applications" and thus what developers need to be able to deliver.


Full-Stack JavaScript is the Best Way to Learn Web Development!

Not only is full-stack JS where "the puck is moving," it is also the best way to learn how to code in general. Modern web development requires keeping many different parts of the stack in your mind at the same time. The World's largest and most cutting-edge companies today want to give users highly interactive and responsive experiences that don't require a page refresh at every step. As a result, the web of today is dominated by "Single Page Applications" written in JavaScript using frameworks like AngularJS.

Dynamic industry-driven curriculum

Our curriculum is constantly evolving in response to feedback from our employers and staff, while holding true to the principal goal of graduating top developers. Our graduates will be able to quickly and independently learn any new technologies, languages or tools that a specific job requires, even those they've never been exposed to before.

Simulate real job environments

The curriculum has a strong focus on all of the skills required of Software Engineers today, including effective communication, workflow management, product development and implementation, application deployment, and team dynamics. Students are exposed to broken tests, incomplete documentation and other surprises that give them experience with the sorts of real-world challenges that many developers encounter.

Getting accepted to Ward 5 means you've shown the passion for technology and software development. We select individuals like we would to be part of our own startups.

Hungry to learn

For students to realize the full potential of the program they need to invest extraordinary effort. Ward 5 challenges students to dedicate themselves to their growth with more time and energy than many full-time jobs would ever require, to learn how to grow through personal challenges and to constantly adapt to the unpredictable.

Relentless pursuit of curiosity

Students and our world class team of Engineers, educators and professionals are passionate about our mission to transform education. That excitement is contagious: we are often inspired to work 80+ hours every week of the program.

Dedication and Teamwork

Before applying to Ward 5, applicants should be prepared to dedicate themselves entirely to their education and its community: 8+ hours a day, 5 days a week for 13 weeks. If you have questions, we encourage you to contact our team.
