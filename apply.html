Ward 5 Application

We're excited that you are applying to Ward 5. Ward 5's admissions process takes about 2-3 weeks from application to decision. Admissions are done on a rolling basis, so the earlier you apply, the better!

Course Preparation

ADMISSIONS PROCESS

  • Application Submission: You submit your complete Ward 5 application.
  • In-Person Interview: Depending on your assessment outcome, we invite you to an in-person behavioral & technical Skype interview.
  • Assessment: During the interview process we will ask you to pull up some prior code you've written. This could be from codecademy, codeschool, books, etc. We'll ask you describe what you did and why. We won't ask you about testing!
  • Optional Follow Up Questions: If necessary we invite you to another in-person or Skype interview.
  • Decision: We deliver an admissions decision to you, and talk about next-steps.

Please contact us at admissions@ward5.io if you have any other questions; we look forward to reading your application.
